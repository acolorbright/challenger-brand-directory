Challenger Brand Directory

A living directory of challenger brand concepts across a wide range of markets. Browse the norms each category takes for granted, then compare four different ways a challenger brand could respond.

We don't have a sweet spot for an industry but for a certain type of organization: challengers. Obsessed with challenger brands, we developed a framework to identify potential niches in markets.

The idea is simple: identify the norms in an industry, then imagine how a brand could iterate on or redefine what matters and push or counter what's considered good.
